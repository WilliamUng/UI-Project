Legend of Zelda: Breath of The Wild

Developer(s): Nintendo EPD

Publisher(s): Nintendo

Platform(s): Nintendo Switch, Wii U

Release Date: March 3, 2017

Series: The Legend of Zelda

Genre: Action-Adventure

Ratings: 10/10-IGN, 10/10-GameSpot

Description:The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild[a] is an action-adventure game developed and published by Nintendo. An entry in the longrunning The Legend of Zelda series, it was released for the Nintendo Switch and Wii U consoles on March 3, 2017. Breath of the Wild is set at the end of the series' timeline; the player controls Link, who awakens from a hundred-year slumber to defeat Calamity Ganon before it can destroy the kingdom of Hyrule.
